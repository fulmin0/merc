# Medication and Executive Function

Anyone considering medication for ADHD should speak with a psychiatrist or GP with ADHD experience.

Two things worth knowing for context:

- **It works while active, reverts when stopped.** Effects are state-dependent — not a permanent change. This is consistent across the stimulant literature. [Tamminga et al., 2016 — *Psychological Medicine*, PubMed 27019103]
- **It does not replace behavioural strategies.** Solanto et al. (2010) found that medication status did not affect outcomes in MCT — the skill-building worked equally with or without stimulants. The two are additive, not substitutes. [PMC3633586]
