**Key finding from research:**
Dawson/Guare explicitly based their 12-skill framework on Barkley's 1997 model, not an independent theory. Confirmed via ESQ-R validation study (PMC8430784) and PMC book review (PMC2542929). The 12 skills are practitioner-facing observational categories, not neurologically distinct constructs.

**Hierarchy established:** Miyake (cognitive science) → Barkley 1997 (neuropsychological theory) → D/G 12 skills + Barkley 2011 BDEFS (parallel applied elaborations of the same theory)
